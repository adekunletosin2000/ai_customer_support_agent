# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Cost**: ₹50 for orders under ₹500, FREE for orders above ₹500
- **Tracking**: Tracking number provided within 24 hours of shipment

## Express Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days  
- **Cost**: ₹150 flat rate
- **Tracking**: Real-time tracking available

## Same-Day Delivery
- **Availability**: Select cities only (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore)
- **Delivery Time**: Order before 12 PM for same-day delivery
- **Cost**: ₹200
- **Cutoff**: Orders placed after 12 PM will be delivered next day

## International Shipping
- Currently not available
- Coming soon to select countries

## Shipping Delays
- During peak season (festivals, sales), delivery may take 1-2 extra days
- We'll notify you via email if there's any delay in your order

# Returns & Refunds Policy

## Return Window
- **30 days** from date of delivery for most products
- **7 days** for electronics and wearables
- Products must be unused and in original packaging

## Eligible for Return
- Damaged or defective products
- Wrong item received
- Product not as described
- Manufacturing defects

## Not Eligible for Return
- Products without original tags or packaging
- Used or worn products
- Personalized or customized items  
- Intimate apparel or hygiene products

## Refund Process
1. Initiate return request from "My Orders"
2. Select reason for return
3. Upload photos (if product damaged)
4. We'll send a prepaid return label within 24 hours
5. Ship the product back using the label
6. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the item

## Refund Methods
- Original payment method (credit/debit card, UPI)
- Store credit (instant, + 10% bonus)
- Bank transfer (3-5 business days)

## Exchange Policy
- Free exchange available for size/color issues
- Exchange processed within 3-5 business days
- Subject to product availability

# Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods

### Cards
- Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Rupay
- Debit Cards: All major banks supported
- **No extra charges**

### Digital Wallets
- Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay
- Amazon Pay
- **Instant confirmation**

### UPI
- Any UPI ID supported
- QR code payment available
- **Real-time payment**

### Net Banking
- All major banks
- Secure 3D authentication

### Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Available for orders under ₹10,000
- Additional ₹50 charge
- Available in most serviceable areas

### EMI Options
- 3, 6, 9, 12 months EMI available
- Minimum order value: ₹3,000
- Zero interest on major banks

## Payment Security
- All transactions encrypted with 256-bit SSL
- PCI DSS compliant
- No payment information stored on our servers

## Failed Payment
- Amount will be refunded to source within 3-5 business days
- You can retry payment or choose different method
- Contact bank if amount debited but order not placed
